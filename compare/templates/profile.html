About Me

My name is Phillip Ashe; I've had a lifelong interest in computers, and have been programming since high school. I live in Western Massachusetts, about 70 minutes from Boston. Although I've primarily worked with Java, C, and C++, I've found myself using Perl and PHP in my professional life. I'm constantly applying myself to learn new technologies, so at a personal level, I've been learning the Django framework and OpenCV. I have a strong interest in Machine Learning and AI, and a strong interest in web backend development. Those two interests culminated in the project Tomify.

If it's not readily apparent, I'm a HUGE Boston sports fan. With his 6th Super Bowl victory, I felt it best to commemorate Tom Brady, the greatest to ever play the game, by incorporating him into my latest project.

GO PATS!
